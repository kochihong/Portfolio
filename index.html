Case Studies

Case Study 1: Teaching Online platform

Under our class module, students worked in teams of 5 to co-design an online platform for a niche target group over 13 weeks. Our team chose to target the teaching community due to the accessibility and feasibility for usability testing, as well as relevance during the ongiong 2021 pandemic.

Design project 1

As part of a sustainability initiative, I designed the prototype for a DIY reusable cup holder that can be made using everday materials. The design is simplistic which allows easy usage by the common public. After our pilot test, 90% of respondents who participated expressed that they would be willing to continue using the product. The design was also awarded "Best Project - Prototype" by NUS Ridge View Residential College (RVRC).
